Typography & Colors

Standard text looks like this. It should be legible and comfortable. To truly test the line-height configuration, we need a longer paragraph that wraps multiple lines. Good typography is about the rhythm of the language as it flows down the page, ensuring that the reader's eye can easily track from the end of one line to the beginning of the next without fatigue. The vertical space between lines (leading) plays a crucial role in this, as does the measure (line length).

这是一段中文长文本测试。在设计排版时，中文字符的密度通常比英文更高，因此需要更宽松的行高来保证呼吸感。如果行高设置得当，大段文字看起来会像是一块块整齐的织物，既紧凑又有序。反之，如果行距过小，文字会挤在一起，令人感到压抑；如果过大，视线跳跃时容易产生断裂感。优雅的排版应该是在“透气”与“凝聚”之间找到完美的平衡。
